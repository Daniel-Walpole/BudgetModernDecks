About Me

Hello, my name is Daniel. I am a level 1 judge for Magic the Gathering ™ from the central Ohio area. I want to give a guide to people who want to get into the modern format and make it seem like it's accessable to all.

Modern can be very expensive and some of the people don't get it that a large portion of the people in this world can't just put one-thousand dollars down to make a new modern deck, and then upkeep it with every new card that comes out every set.

My favorite decks to play are Red/Green Stompy and anything with either Through the Breech or Goryo's Vengence. Before it's banning I loved to play Narset Cannon with out a sideboard as none of the cards really mattered anyways. The point of this is I love modern it's a great format with a ton of variety.

Anyways I really just want people to play the format that I like and try new cards even if they want without breaking the bank. I also want to make a decks that have a clear plan to evolve over a few months with a few bucks to make them even more potent in the format.
